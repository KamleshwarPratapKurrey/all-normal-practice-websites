​..Athleisure

Gradually the demand for fashionable workout clothes increased. Extremely comfortable yoga pants to unique sports bras for different activities evolved with graphic patterns. 2010 was almost the starting point for fashion to become casual. Wellness movements of staying fit, eating healthy and going to the gym at least once a day became a range around that time and that in turn, boosted the athleisure trend. Social media platforms are also to be credited for boosting the same as they gave the much needed push to the new fashion trend.
